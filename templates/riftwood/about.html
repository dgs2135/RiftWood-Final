About RiftWood

Custom Instruments, Honest Craft

🎯 Our Mission

To build instruments that meet you where you are—affordable, reliable, and fully custom. Whether you're chasing tone, testing new setups, or finally upgrading from your starter gear, we're here to help you shape your sound your way.

🪵 Where It All Started

We didn’t start in a factory—we started in a shed.

Back in 1983, in a quiet countryside town in Japan, a furniture craftsman named Hiroshi Tanaka decided to make a guitar from scratch for his son. It wasn’t perfect, but it meant everything. That one guitar changed the course of his workshop—and his life.

He shifted from building chairs to building instruments, one order at a time. Musicians in the area began to take notice. A few years later, Hiroshi and his family relocated to San Jose, California, bringing his tools, techniques, and philosophy with him.

In San Jose, word spread fast—through garage bands, college jam circles, and local shops. Hiroshi’s builds were different. They weren’t flashy. They were honest. Well-built. Custom-feeling without the custom-shop price tag.

That was the beginning of something bigger.

Every instrument is made-to-order, with the same heart Hiroshi put into that first guitar. No gimmicks. Just honest craftsmanship and deep customization—for musicians who care.

🧰 What We Offer

  • Fully customizable guitars, basses, and drums
  • Detailed online configurators that let you tweak everything: pickups, finishes, hardware, tonewood, scale length—you name it
  • Clear, flexible pricing that doesn’t sacrifice build quality
  • Options built for modders, tweakers, and tone-chasers

Whether you're upgrading from your first rig or chasing a dream build that won’t wreck your budget, we’ve got you.
